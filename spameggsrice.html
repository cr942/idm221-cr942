Spam, Eggs, & Rice

This is my favorite thing to eat and not just for breakfast! In this recipe, we are making plain spam into something great and tasty!

Ingredients:

  • Spam
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • Optional:
    • Furikake seasoning
    • Sushi nori

Directions:

  1. Wash and cook 1 cup of rice (1 cup per serving).
  2. In a small bowl, mix in brown sugar, soy sauce, and mirin. Add as much soy sauce and mirin in an even ratio to how much spam you are cooking/how much sauce you would like! (I prefer to have a glaze instead of full-on sauce).
  3. Slice the spam into half-inch thick strips (or you can dice them).
  4. Add cooking spray to a medium-sized pan and place on medium heat.
  5. Throw in your spam pieces and cook evenly for about 3 minutes.
  6. Pour your sauce mixture into the pan with the spam, and mix to coat it evenly. Let it cook for about another 2 minutes.
  7. Prepare a bowl of cooked rice and mix in furikake seasoning.
  8. Take out the spam and place it on top of the rice. Drizzle any excess sauce from the pan on top (or you can save it for the eggs).
  9. In the same pan (or a separate pan if you donâ€™t want the sauce in your eggs/are saving the sauce for on top), fry your eggs until they are cooked to your preference.
  10. Place the eggs on top of your spam.
  11. Enjoy by itself or with sushi nori!
